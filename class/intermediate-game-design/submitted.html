it's to make a short (3-4 minute) pitch presentation for a game of your own design, to be given next week -- it can be anything you want, whatever ideas are exciting you right now. you can work solo, or in pairs, though it has to be a new game (i.e. not something you are already working on!). this is your oppotunity to dream a game into existence, with no obligations to ever create it! i encourage you to follow what you are passionate about. this is your game! you can make anything!!

as for your presentation, there's 20 of us so do try to keep it to 3-4 minutes, and focus on the "what" and the "why." gives us a concrete picture of what your game is, what's interesting or special about it. don't worry about budget, scope, team, production details etc -- just focus on the core concept / idea!

some tips :)

remember to include basic info about your game (like is it single- or multiplayer), as well as gameplay, art style, narrative, etc.

i encourage you to include at least one specific gameplay example for your game -- it just helps the audience really understand the particulars of your design, and helps us to understand if your design is going to work or not (help us "see it in action").

you want some text to help us understand the high level ideas, but not too much (like blocks) of text -- make sure you aren't just reading off your slides!

have fun :)
