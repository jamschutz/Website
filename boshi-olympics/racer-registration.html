stats explanation:

speed: this is how fast your character will go each time you cheer them on.

stamina: this is how many times you can cheer your character before they get tired, and their speed decreases by 90%. (note that speed resets at the beginning of the NEXT race)

determination: when your stamina runs out, this is the % chance it will automatically refill and your speed instead goes up by 120%
